## Notes for discussion

- Ruben does **not** coach these sessions; the hired coach runs the group.
- **6 players** uses two courts at the same time (higher court cost, same or lower pot than 5 on 1h).
- Amounts can be rounded in practice (e.g. €22 / €14 instead of €21.60 / €14.40).
- If the club charges more than €10 for 1h30 on a single court, subtract the difference from the pot before splitting.
